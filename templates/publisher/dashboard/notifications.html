Change notifications

When a tracked script's source repository changes, WikiScriptSync posts a message to your Wikimedia talk page so you know to review and update.

How this works: After you publish a script, it is tracked automatically. A dedicated bot account checks for upstream changes and posts to your talk page on {{ notifier_bot_wiki }} as user {{ notifier_bot_display }}. You decide whether to publish the new version — nothing is changed automatically.
